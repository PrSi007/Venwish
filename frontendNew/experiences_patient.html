Project VenWish

Ven is Danish word which means friend or someone you care for. A charitable program for people from different backgrounds . “Let’s come together and get active in process of change”




Be the reason someone believes in the goodness of people.

As John Green says in his best selling novel ‘Fault in our stars’- “Apparently the world is not a wish granting factory” .So in our project VenWish we aim to create a wish granting machine where people who are fighting for their life can express their desires or wishes close to their heart and others can donate to fulfill their dreams.




A kind gesture can reach a wound that only compassion can heal.

Helping hands is better than praying lips . We cannot do all the good that the world needs,but the world needs all the good that we can do . Be that good to someone as extend your helping hand and be an angel in someone’s life.




What mental health needs is more sunlight, more candor, and more unashamed conversation.

Our website will provide a “sharing circle platform” to people who are going through a difficult phase in their life- A safe space where they can get loose, let their guard down and talk/interact freely with friends. These conservations will enable a thought process in which they will gain strength from each other’s story. The sharing circles can be an amazing exercise to connect with each other.
